Configure a new session (topic filter + question count + include memorized toggle). Below: session history with date, topic, score.
ACTIVE SESSION /sessions/[id]
Full-screen study mode. No sidebar. Shows question → reveal answer → mark correct/wrong. Progress bar. Repeats wrong answers at end.
